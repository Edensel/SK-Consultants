Unlocking Enterprise Growth in Emerging Markets

Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) are often touted as the backbone of African economies, yet many struggle to scale beyond the micro-enterprise level. The "missing middle" phenomenon persists due to a combination of internal operational inefficiencies and external market barriers.

The Scalability Challenge

While many businesses start with strong entrepreneurial energy, they often hit a ceiling caused by informal structures. Operating without clear financial records, standardized processes, or strategic plans limits their ability to attract investment or manage larger contracts.

Strategic Levers for Growth

At KB & Associates, we focus on three critical pillars for SME growth:

  • Operational Excellence: Streamlining workflows and adopting digital tools to reduce waste and improve delivery times.
  • Governance & Structure: separating ownership from management to build professional, resilient organizations.
  • Market Linkages: Integrating SMEs into larger value chains where they can secure stable demand.

By addressing these foundational elements, businesses can transition from survival mode to sustainable expansion, creating jobs and driving local economic development.
