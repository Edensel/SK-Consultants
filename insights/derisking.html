The Capital Conundrum

East Africa is home to some of the world's fastest-growing economies, yet the "missing middle"—SMEs too big for microfinance but too risky for commercial banks—remains a persistent challenge. Investors are eager but wary of perceived risks.

De-risking is not about eliminating risk (which is impossible) but about sharing and managing it intelligently to catalyze investment.

Blended Finance

Blended finance—the strategic use of development finance to mobilize private capital—is proving to be a game-changer. By using public funds to provide first-loss guarantees or technical assistance facilities, we can crowd in private investors who would otherwise stay on the sidelines.

The Role of Data

Often, the perceived risk is actually an information gap. KB & Associates is leveraging alternative data scoring models to prove the creditworthiness of MSMEs, reducing the reliance on traditional collateral.

As we move forward, the integration of digital financial services will continue to lower the cost of due diligence and monitoring, making smaller ticket sizes viable for global investors.
