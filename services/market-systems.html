Market Systems Development

Building resilience in complex economic environments.

Home / Services / Market Systems

Systemic Change for Lasting Impact

Market Systems Development (MSD) focuses on addressing the root causes of market failure rather than just the symptoms. We analyze the entire value chain—from production to consumption—to identify bottlenecks and opportunities for leverage.

Our goal is to create systems that are inclusive, competitive, and resilient to shocks such as climate change or economic downturns.

Strategic Focus Areas

  • Value Chain Analysis: Mapping the flow of goods, services, and information to optimize efficiency.
  • Business Continuity Planning: Preparing businesses to survive and thrive during crises.
  • Cross-Border Trade: Facilitating regional integration and removing non-tariff barriers to trade.
  • Climate-Smart Agriculture: integrating sustainable practices into agricultural market systems.

Approach

We use a facilitative approach, working through local partners and market actors to ensure that changes are owned locally and sustained long after our intervention ends.
